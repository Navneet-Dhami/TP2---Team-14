About Us

Who We Are?

The Soundwaves Team consists of 10 determined, productive university students. All engineers, aspiring for nothing less than greatness! With our diverse skills & experiences alongside our strong interest in music - we've come together to share our vision of how amazing sound can be! Experience The Soundwaves Baby!

Our Mission.

Our aim is to provide high quality music products which will enhance the experience of our customers as they enjoy the finely tuned soundwaves from our products as they listen to their favourite musical artists, bands, group admin and everything in-between!

We seek to elevate any occasion - party, study session, gym class - You name it!
